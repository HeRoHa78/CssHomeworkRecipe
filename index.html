Simple and delicious APPLE cake

Ingredients Amount
Eggs 4
Oil 12 tbsp
Sugar 12 tbsp
Flour 12 tbsp
Baking powder 2 tsp
Apples 1,5 kg
  1. Step: Beat the eggs with a whisk. Add oil to the foam. Stir. Mix the flour with baking powder and sugar, and add to the egg-oil mixture. Finally, add apple pieces.
  2. Step: To make the cake juicy, you can add enough pieces of apple to blacken the dough. Mix all the dough well and pour it on a large baking sheet. Then shake sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Step: Bake at 200 degrees for ~30-40 minutes until the cake is golden brown.
